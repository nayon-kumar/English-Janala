FrequentlyAsked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website offers many free lessons and resources for learners. However, some advanced courses or special features may require a premium subscription.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You can access some basic content without creating an account. However, signing up allows you to track your progress, save your lessons, and access additional features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily with our word lists, interactive exercises, quizzes, and reading materials. We also recommend using new words in sentences to improve retention.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we offer certificates for certain completed courses. After successfully finishing the required lessons and assessments, you will be able to download your certificate.
